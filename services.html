Collision

The first thoughts after you are involved in an auto collision are often filled with fear, confusion and what to do next. The amazing staff and crew over at TJs Towing are determined to help you navigate your way through the sometimes-daunting task of what happens after you have been involved in a wreck. A friendly face and knowing that someone is really there to help you along the way can sometimes mean the world after you have experienced the sometimes traumatizing but always stressful incident of an auto collision.

Step 1

After the auto collision occurs, ensure that you and all members involved are not physically injured if an injury applies contact ems services and tend to the parties needs to the best of your abilities until help arrives. If no one is injured and there is no damage to private, municipal or highway property take plenty of pictures of the vehicles than steer it the cars off the road way to continue step 2. If anyone is injured or if u suspect any criminal activity proceed to step 4.

Step 2

You and the other parties involved in the collision are going to have to exchange documentation which includes your license, ownership and insurance. The simplest way to do this is either take a picture of each of the documents or fill this template out.

Step 3

If you require a tow call us @ 647-764-1991 and we will arrive to tow your vehicle directly to report your collision, if your vehicle is safe to drive you will be required to attend a Collision Reporting Center within 24 hours of the time of the collision.

Step 4

A police officer will have to attend your collision in this case ensure that you are in a safe spot to await the officers arrival. Once the officer finishes his investigation, we are able to assist in towing your vehicle to a destination of your choice.

We here at TJs Towing understand that having your car towed can often seem like a headache but if you keep in mind that a damaged vehicle is safer off the road until serviced and that you have rights and know what they are, it will help it move along smoother. These rights are as follows:

  • Who tows your car, and where they tow it to is your choice. Unless otherwise instructed by the police or fire department.
  • A tow form giving them permission to tow the vehicle must be signed before it is towed. Unless you belong to an auto club.
  • You must have a detailed invoice before any payment is required or collected from the towing company.
  • The final bill for the tow cannot be any higher than 10 percent of the price that was quoted to you.
  • You have the right to pay by credit card.
  • You have the right to access any personal belongings that are in the vehicle while it is at the towing business, as long as it is done during business hours.
  • You must be notified when your vehicle is going to be towed.
  • The towing company has to tell you if they are getting an incentive for towing your vehicle to a certain tow shop or body shop.

At any time feel free to contact us with any questions in regards to the above content as it is TJs Towing’s goal to make sure we are with you every step of the way, and to make it as smooth of a process as we can, furthermore ensuring that you are provided exactly what you need to get you back on the road.
